Christmas 2020 - 2020-12-25

In the immortal words of Judy Garland:

Have yourself a merry little Christmas
Let your heart be light
Next year all our troubles will be out of sight

Have yourself a merry little Christmas
Make the yuletide gay
Next year all our troubles will be miles away

Once again as in olden days
Happy golden days of yore
Faithful friends who were near to us
Will be dear to us once more

Someday soon we all will be together
If the fates allow
Until then, we'll have to muddle through somehow
So have yourself a merry little Christmas now

There's been so much awesomeness in the Perl community over the past year that I'd like to thank each and every one involved for making the best of things. For example, I really enjoyed Conference In The Cloud. Despite not being able to see my friends and members of the community in person, I got to speak at a conference without the usual hassle of flying and being away from my family.

Thank you one and all.

So until next year, or Someday soon when we will all be together I wish you the very happiest of Christmas.
